About me!

Hello! Let me introduce myself.

My name is Caylin Hirapara, and I am currently located in Clemson, SC.

While earning my B.A. in English at Clemson University, my interests shifted toward professional writing and graphic design. I didn’t have to look far to find the Clemson University Master of Arts in Writing, Rhetoric, and Media . I chose this program because it enabled me to pursue interdisciplinary studies and create my own areas of specialization: Technical Communication, User Experience Research & Design (UX) and Content Strategy. I was also able to study information design, business writing, visual rhetoric, and rhetorical theory.

About the Creation of this Site

I created this portfolio for my Master’s defense in the Spring of 2020. After looking through Word press, Wix, and Squarespace, I set out to learn to create a html website site from scratch. With my limited knowledge of html, I worked with a free template from https://freehtml5.co/. Throughout the process, with much trial and error, I learned the basics of html editing. I created a logo for myself using Adobe Spark. My biggest challenge came in creating a dropdown navigation toolbar for the site which was not in the original template. Using articles from w3schools.com, I discovered the bootstrap and .js files could customize the site. I created my own CSS navigation style customized to match the rest of the site. The drop down allowed for the site to be navigated. I purchased the Caylin.me domain to give the site easy accessibility and began transferring all my projects onto the site.
